Our Story

Built One Weld
at a Time

Zuwa Steel Works was founded with one mission — to give Nigerians steel fabrication that doesn't force them to choose between security and beauty. A gate, a window bar or a railing can be both impenetrable and a work of art.

Over 15 years we have completed 500+ projects across Nigeria — from private homes in Lagos to business premises in Abuja and Port Harcourt. Every job gets our full dedication and the same obsessive attention to detail.

We work with the highest grade steel, employ master craftsmen, and never cut corners. That is the Zuwa Steel promise — and we have never broken it.
